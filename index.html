London

London is the colorful and history-rich capital of England. Known for its historic buildings, museums, theaters, and green parks along the River Thames. Recognized for landmarks such as Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, and Tower Bridge, this cosmopolitan city attracts millions of visitors each year with its cultural diversity and dynamic atmosphere.

Paris

Paris is the capital of France and is known as the capital of romance, art, and culture. It is famous for iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and the Champs-Élysées. With its sidewalk cafes, fashion houses, and historic buildings, it attracts millions of tourists every year.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the dynamic and modern capital of Japan. It is renowned for its high-tech industry, neon-lit streets, traditional temples, and vibrant street markets. Tokyo is one of the world's largest metropolises, where different cultures and lifestyles can be explored in every corner.
